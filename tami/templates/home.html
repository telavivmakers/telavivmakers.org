Welcome to TAMI! We are the largest and most active hackerspace and makerspace in Israel. Our community provides members and visitors with the neccesary tools, knowldge and help to create, hack, make and build their next project. Our collective interests are in woodworking, electronics, cooking, programming, sewing, welding, 3D printing and anything else that comes to mind.
